Quick Answers

Frequently Asked Questions

Get instant answers to common questions about our courses and services

Is the PDC certification globally recognized?

Yes, our Permaculture Design Certificate follows the 72-hour international standard established by Bill Mollison. Our graduates are recognized by design institutes worldwide and can practice permaculture design professionally across the globe.

Do I need previous experience?

No! We have courses ranging from total beginners to professional-grade design workshops. Our introductory courses assume no prior knowledge, while advanced courses build upon foundational concepts. We'll guide you from your first seed to your first masterplan.

How long do I have to complete courses?

Most courses offer lifetime access with flexible pacing. The PDC course typically takes 3-6 months part-time, but you can take up to 12 months if needed. We believe in learning at your own rhythm, not rushing your connection with nature.

What's included in the course materials?

All courses include video lessons, downloadable PDFs, practical assignments, design templates, and access to our community forum. Premium courses also include one-on-one mentoring sessions and project reviews.
