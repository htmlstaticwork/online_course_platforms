Course Description

Permaculture Food Forest Design is more than just organic gardening. It is a systematic design approach that creates high-yield, low-maintenance landscapes by mimicking the structural layers and ecological relationships of a natural forest.

In this comprehensive masterclass, Oliver Green takes you through the step-by-step process of transitioning any landscape—from a small backyard to a large farm—into a thriving, self-fertilizing food forest.

What you'll learn:

  • 7 Layers of a Food Forest
  • Guild Design & Companion Planting
  • Soil Building & Fungal Networks
  • Nutrient Cycling & Mulching

Requirements:

  • Basic interest in sustainable living
  • A small patch of earth (optional)
  • Beginner design mindset
